Methodology & Limitations:
• Data: Nifty 5-min OHLCV via Yahoo Finance (^NSEI) — last ~60 calendar days · India VIX daily (^INDIAVIX) for implied volatility
• Option Pricing: Black-Scholes using India VIX as IV — approximation only; actual premiums differ due to skew/smile, especially for OTM strikes
• Strike: 15M SuperTrend level rounded to nearest 50 · Expiry: Current week Thursday (next week if signal fires on Thursday)
• Entry: 5M Nifty close above 15M ST (bull flip) → Sell PE · Bear flip → Sell CE · Premium recorded at signal bar open
• Exit Rule 1: 15M ST flips back (direction reversal) · Exit Rule 2: 5M ST on Nifty flips against position direction
• Commission: ₹40 per side (₹80 round trip) · P&L = (Entry Premium − Exit Premium) × Lot Size × Lots − Commission
