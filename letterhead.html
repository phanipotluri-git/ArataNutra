01 🦠 WSSV — తెల్ల మచ్చ వైరస్ రొయ్య రోగం
Cause: WSSV — dsDNA virus. OIE Notifiable. Patil et al. 2021: India ₹1,670 crore/yr loss; 2.58 t/ha/crop loss — highest of all shrimp diseases. Transmission: infected seed, wild crustaceans, birds, untreated water, shared equipment. No treatment exists.
Signs / Symptoms
White spots (0.5–2 mm) on carapace, visible under bright light. Loose shell. Pink-reddish discolouration. Shrimp crowding at surface. Reduced feeding 1–2 days before mass die-off. Total mortality within 3–10 days.
Prevention — only defence
  • Use only SPF/PCR-certified PL (CIBA/MPEDA hatcheries)MandatoryRequest WSSV+EHP+IHHNV PCR certificate with every batch
  • Complete pond dry 7–14 days + CaO200–300 kg/acreKills WSSV reservoir hosts in bottom. Raises pH to 11+
  • Filter all inlet water — 300 µm mesh + chlorinate30 ppm Cl₂Wild crabs/shrimp = WSSV carriers. Neutralise with Na₂S₂O₃ before stocking
  • Bird exclusion nets over full pondMandatoryHerons, egrets: confirmed mechanical WSSV vectors between ponds
  • β-glucan + Vitamin C in feed (DOC 15 onwards)3–5 + 2 g/kgBoosts innate immune response — slows WSSV progression when challenged
WSSV confirmed — emergency response
  • Harvest immediately if marketable size (>8 g)Total or partial100% mortality within 5–7 days after confirmation. Harvest now.
  • After harvest: drain + BKC+TCCA disinfect + dry ≥10 days5 ppm eachDo NOT rush re-stocking after WSSV crop.
✖ NO ANTIVIRAL TREATMENT EXISTSPrevention is the only strategy: SPF seed + biosecurity + water disinfection. All chemical treatments during active WSSV are futile.
কারণ: WSSV — dsDNA ভাইরাস। ভারতে ₹১,৬৭০ কোটি/বছর ক্ষতি। ছড়ানোর পথ: সংক্রমিত পোনা, বন্য কাঁকড়া/চিংড়ি, পাখি, দূষিত সরঞ্জাম। কোনো চিকিৎসা নেই।
Signs / Symptoms
খোলে ও শরীরে সাদা দাগ। আলগা খোল। গোলাপি রঙ। পৃষ্ঠে ভিড়। মৃত্যুর ১–২ দিন আগে খাবার বন্ধ। ৩–১০ দিনে সম্পূর্ণ মৃত্যু।
নিবারণ — একমাত্র প্রতিরক্ষা
  • শুধুমাত্র SPF/PCR সার্টিফাইড পোনাঅবশ্যইCIBA/MPEDA হ্যাচারি থেকে। PCR সার্টিফিকেট চান।
  • পুকুর ৭–১৪ দিন শুকানো + কলিচুন২০০–৩০০ কেজি/একর
  • ৩০০ µm জাল ছাঁকনি + ৩০ ppm Cl₂ ক্লোরিনেশনঅবশ্যইমজুতের আগে Na₂S₂O₃ দিয়ে নিরপদ করুন।
  • পাখি রোধক জাল — পুরো পুকুরঅবশ্যই
  • β-গ্লুকান + ভিটামিন সি খাবারে (DOC ১৫+)৩–৫ + ২ গ্রা/কেজি
WSSV নিশ্চিত হলে
  • বিক্রয়যোগ্য হলে অবিলম্বে ধরুন>৮ গ্রাম হলে৫–৭ দিনে ১০০% মৃত্যু হবে। এখনই ধরুন।
  • ধরার পরে পুকুর সম্পূর্ণ জীবাণুমুক্ত করুনBKC+TCCA ৫ ppm
✖ কোনো অ্যান্টিভাইরাল চিকিৎসা নেইনিবারণই একমাত্র কৌশল। প্রাদুর্ভাবের সময় রাসায়নিক চিকিৎসা বৃথা।
కారణం: WSSV — dsDNA వైరస్. భారత్‌లో ₹1,670 కోటి/సంవత్సరం నష్టం. వ్యాప్తి: కలుషిత పిల్లలు, అడవి పీతలు/రొయ్యలు, పక్షులు, పరికరాలు. చికిత్స లేదు.
Signs / Symptoms
పెచ్చుపై తెల్ల మచ్చలు. వదులైన పెచ్చు. గులాబీ రంగు. నీటిపై రొయ్యలు. 1–2 రోజుల ముందే తినడం ఆగుతుంది. 3–10 రోజుల్లో మొత్తం మరణాలు.
నివారణ — ఏకైక రక్షణ
  • SPF/PCR సర్టిఫైడ్ పిల్లలు మాత్రమేతప్పనిసరిCIBA/MPEDA హ్యాచరీ. PCR సర్టిఫికెట్ అడగండి.
  • 7–14 రోజులు ఆరబెట్టి CaO వేయండి200–300 కేజీ/ఎకరా
  • 300 µm జాలి + 30 ppm Cl₂ క్లోరినేషన్తప్పనిసరిNa₂S₂O₃ తో నిర్వీర్యం చేయండి.
  • పక్షి-వల — పూర్తి చెరువు కవరేజ్తప్పనిసరి
  • β-గ్లూకాన్ + విటమిన్ C మేతలో (DOC 15+)3–5 + 2 గ్రా/కేజీ
WSSV నిర్ధారిస్తే — అత్యవసరం
  • వెంటనే పట్టండి (>8 గ్రా ఉంటే)+8 గ్రా ఉంటే5–7 రోజుల్లో 100% మరణాలు. ఇప్పుడే పట్టండి.
  • పంట తర్వాత మొత్తం శుద్ధి + ≥10 రోజులు ఆరబెట్టండిBKC+TCCA 5 ppm
✖ యాంటీవైరల్ చికిత్స లేదునివారణ మాత్రమే వ్యూహం. ముప్పు సమయంలో రసాయన చికిత్స పూర్తిగా వ్యర్థం.
02🔬EHP — Enterocytozoon hepatopenaei (Microsporidian)#1 ECONOMIC LOSS INDIA
02🔬ইএইচপি — হেপাটোপ্যানক্রিয়াস পরজীবী
02🔬EHP — కాలేయ పాన్క్రియాస్ పరాన్నజీవి
Cause: EHP microsporidian spore parasite invades hepatopancreatic tubule cells, destroying nutrient absorption. Patil et al. 2021: India ₹3,977 crore/yr — exceeds WSSV loss. 17% farm probability in AP. Spreads via feces in pond water. No approved cure — prevention and management only.
Signs / Symptoms
Earliest sign: White floating feces on pond surface. Severe size variation ("big-small mix") in same pond. Pale/yellowish hepatopancreas. Soft shell. Empty midgut. FCR >2.5 despite normal feeding. Weight gain stops.
Prevention — pond prep is the most critical step
  • EHPF-certified PL — PCR hepatopancreas test before stockingMandatoryEHP transmits vertically (broodstock→larvae) and horizontally (feces in water). Test every batch arriving at farm.
  • Full drying 7–14 days + CaO — destroys EHP spores in mud200–300 kg/acreEHP spores survive months in bottom sediment. Only full drying + lime destroys them. Rushed prep = EHP carryover.
  • Daily probiotics in water — Bacillus spp. from DOC 110⁵–10⁶ CFU/mlCompetitive exclusion reduces EHP spore load in water. Never skip — discontinuation loses effect within 48 hrs.
  • Organic acid in feed (butyric/formic acid) — every day3–5 g/kg feedReduces spore shedding in feces. Improves gut integrity. Must be daily — intermittent use is ineffective.
EHP-positive crop management
  • Confirm by PCR first — do not assume from field signs aloneHP tissue PCR
  • Harvest early DOC 80–90 — EHP shrimp never recover growthPartial or full harvestCalculate break-even DOC before stocking. Early harvest at commercial size limits total loss.
  • Next crop: reduce stocking density 20–30%Max 60 PL/m²
⚠ EHP + WSSV CO-INFECTION CONFIRMED IN AP & ODISHACo-infected shrimp have dramatically higher mortality. Any combination of size variation + white spots = PCR for both diseases simultaneously.
কারণ: EHP মাইক্রোস্পোরিডিয়ান পরজীবী হেপাটোপ্যানক্রিয়াস কোষ নষ্ট করে। ভারতে ₹৩,৯৭৭ কোটি/বছর ক্ষতি — WSSV-এর চেয়ে বেশি। ১৭% খামারে হওয়ার সম্ভাবনা। মলের মাধ্যমে ছড়ায়। অনুমোদিত চিকিৎসা নেই।
লক্ষণ
প্রথম চিহ্ন: পুকুরের পৃষ্ঠে সাদা ভাসমান মল। একই পুকুরে বড়-ছোট চিংড়ির মিশ্রণ। ফ্যাকাশে হেপাটোপ্যানক্রিয়াস। আলগা খোল। স্বাভাবিক খাবার সত্ত্বেও FCR >২.৫।
নিবারণ
  • EHPF সার্টিফাইড পোনা — মজুতের আগে PCR পরীক্ষাঅবশ্যইপ্রতিটি ব্যাচ আসার সময় পরীক্ষা করুন।
  • পুকুর পুরো শুকানো + কলিচুন — EHP স্পোর ধ্বংস২০০–৩০০ কেজি/একরEHP স্পোর কাদায় মাসের পর মাস বাঁচে। সম্পূর্ণ শুকানো + উচ্চ মাত্রার চুনই ধ্বংস করে।
  • প্রোবায়োটিক্স — DOC ১ থেকে প্রতিদিন জলে১০⁵–১০⁶ CFU/ml
  • জৈব অ্যাসিড খাবারে (বিউটিরিক/ফর্মিক) — প্রতিদিন৩–৫ গ্রা/কেজি
ইএইচপি-পজিটিভ ক্রপ
  • আগে PCR নিশ্চিত করুনহেপাটোপ্যানক্রিয়াস PCR
  • DOC ৮০–৯০-এ তাড়াতাড়ি ধরুনআংশিক বা সম্পূর্ণ
  • পরবর্তী ক্রপে ঘনত্ব ২০–৩০% কমানসর্বোচ্চ ৬০ PL/m²
⚠ EHP+WSSV সহ-সংক্রমণ এপি ও ওড়িশায় নিশ্চিতদুটি লক্ষণ একসাথে থাকলে দুটির জন্যই PCR পাঠান।
కారణం: EHP కాలేయ పాన్క్రియాస్ కణాలను నాశనం చేసి పోషకాహార శోషణ ఆపుతుంది. ₹3,977 కోటి/సంవత్సరం నష్టం — WSSV కంటే ఎక్కువ. AP లో 17% చెరువులు ప్రభావితం. మలం ద్వారా వ్యాపిస్తుంది. అనుమతించిన చికిత్స లేదు.
లక్షణాలు
మొదటి సంకేతం: చెరువు మీద తెల్ల మలం తేలడం. ఒకే చెరువులో పెద్ద-చిన్న రొయ్యల మిశ్రమం. లేత కాలేయ పాన్క్రియాస్. వదులైన పెచ్చు. సాధారణ మేత ఉన్నా FCR >2.5.
నివారణ
  • EHPF సర్టిఫైడ్ పిల్లలు — PCR పరీక్ష వేయడానికి ముందుతప్పనిసరిప్రతి బ్యాచ్ వచ్చినప్పుడు పరీక్షించండి.
  • 7–14 రోజులు ఆరబెట్టి CaO — EHP స్పోర్లు నాశనం200–300 కేజీ/ఎకరాEHP స్పోర్లు బురదలో నెలల తరబడి బతుకుతాయి. పూర్తి ఆరిపోవడం + అధిక మాత్ర సున్నమే చంపుతుంది.
  • ప్రోబయోటిక్స్ — DOC 1 నుండి రోజువారీ నీటిలో10⁵–10⁶ CFU/ml
  • సేంద్రీయ ఆమ్లం మేతలో (బ్యూటిరిక్/ఫార్మిక్) — రోజువారీ3–5 గ్రా/కేజీ
EHP-పాజిటివ్ పంట
  • ముందు PCR నిర్ధారించండిHP PCR పరీక్ష
  • DOC 80–90 లో తొందరగా పట్టండిపాక్షిక లేదా పూర్తి
  • తర్వాత పంటలో సాంద్రత 20–30% తగ్గించండిగరిష్టం 60 PL/m²

03🧫విబ్రియోసిస్ & AHPND/EMS — యాంటీబయాటిక్-లేని నిర్వహణ
Cause: Vibrio parahaemolyticus (AHPND/EMS — PirB toxin plasmid strains), V. harveyi (luminescence), V. alginolyticus. AHPND in India since 2015 (AP & TN). All proliferate at low pH, high organics, low DO. Consistent probiotics + pH management eliminates the need for antibiotics entirely.
Signs / Symptoms
AHPND (DOC 10–30): Mass mortality. Pale/atrophied hepatopancreas at necropsy. Shrimp at surface, loose shell, empty gut. V. harveyi: Shrimp glow blue-green at night (luminescence). High early-crop mortality.
Prevention — daily water ecology (most important)
  • Maintain pH 7.8–8.5 with morning lime applicationCaO 10–20 kg/acreVibrio doubles every 10 min at pH 7.0 vs every 2 hrs at pH 8.2. pH above 7.8 = single most effective Vibrio suppression.
  • Daily probiotics in water — Bacillus spp. from DOC 110⁵–10⁶ CFU/mlBacillus produces bacteriocins that inhibit Vibrio. Never skip — effect lost within 48 hrs of discontinuation.
  • Probiotics in feed — Bacillus + Lactobacillus blend3–5 g/kg · dailyGut-level Vibrio suppression. Critical for AHPND prevention — reduces HP colonisation.
  • Organic acid in feed (butyric acid) — daily2–3 g/kgLowers gut pH — hostile to Vibrio. Improves gut wall integrity (tight junctions).
  • TCCA after every water exchange1–2 ppmWait 24 hrs after TCCA before adding probiotics — TCCA kills them.
Active Vibriosis — pond response
  • BKC 80% — evening pond treatment1–2 ppm
  • Water exchange 20–30% + TCCA incoming waterFresh treated water
  • Stop feeding 24–48 hrs + increase aerationDO >5 mg/L
✓ ANTIBIOTICS NOT REQUIREDConsistent daily probiotics from DOC 1 + pH above 7.8 + organic acids prevents the vast majority of Vibriosis. AP farms using this protocol report 80–90% reduction in Vibriosis mortality without antibiotics.
কারণ: ভিব্রিও প্যারাহেমোলিটিকাস (AHPND/EMS), ভিব্রিও হার্ভেই। AHPND ২০১৫ থেকে ভারতে। কম pH, বেশি জৈব পদার্থ, কম DO-তে বাড়ে। নিয়মিত প্রোবায়োটিক + pH ব্যবস্থাপনায় অ্যান্টিবায়োটিক সম্পূর্ণ অপ্রয়োজনীয়।
লক্ষণ
AHPND (DOC ১০–৩০): ব্যাপক মৃত্যু। ফ্যাকাশে হেপাটোপ্যানক্রিয়াস। পৃষ্ঠে চিংড়ি, আলগা খোল, খালি অন্ত্র। ভিব্রিও হার্ভেই: রাতে চিংড়ি নীল-সবুজ আলো দেয়।

Why most critical: Boyd, Torrans & Tucker (2023) — DO must be >5 mg/L at all times. Low DO is the trigger for nearly every secondary disease: Vibrio proliferates, H₂S forms, ammonia toxicity rises, immunity collapses. Boyd (1998): aerator HP must scale with growing shrimp biomass. DO crashes worst pre-dawn (4–6 AM), after heavy rain, algal bloom collapse.

05🧪pH, క్షారీయత, అమ్మోనియా & నైట్రైట్ నిర్వహణ
Boyd & Tucker (2012): pH, alkalinity, TAN, nitrite, and H₂S are all interconnected in the nitrogen cycle. Alkalinity buffering prevents pH swings; adequate alkalinity supports nitrification that converts toxic ammonia to less-toxic nitrate. In intensive ponds, feed nitrogen can overwhelm natural buffering, causing cascading failure. Test twice daily: 5 AM + 5 PM.
pH — target 7.8–8.5, daily swing <0.5 units
  • Ca(OH)₂ agricultural lime — evening application50–100 kg/acreApply evening when pH at daily low. NEVER midday — algal photosynthesis + lime spikes pH above 9.5.
  • CaO quicklime — emergency correction50 kg/acre
Alkalinity — target 100–150 mg/L as CaCO₃
  • Dolomite CaMg(CO₃)₂ — long-term maintenance50–100 kg/acreBelow 80 mg/L = pH instability, nitrification failure, moulting failure. Ca+Mg both needed.
  • Sodium bicarbonate NaHCO₃ — rapid correction10–15 kg/acre
Ammonia (TAN <0.5 mg/L) & Nitrite (<0.1 mg/L)
  • Zeolite — ammonia adsorption from water20–30 kg/acre every 3–5 daysReplenish frequently — saturated zeolite releases ammonia. Critical from DOC 40.
  • Water exchange 10–15%/week from DOC 30TCCA-treated waterRemoves dissolved nitrogen and organic matter.

06📉RMS · WFS · LSS — వదులైన పెచ్చు సిండ్రోమ్
RMS (Running Mortality Syndrome): AP & Bengal since 2011. Non-infectious — low DO + high density + organic overload + opportunistic infections. Continuous 1–3% daily loss DOC 35–90. WFS (White Feces Syndrome): EHP, Vibrio, toxic algae or poor feed. LSS: Mineral imbalance, low alkalinity, temperature shock during moulting.
Signs / Symptoms
RMS: 1–3% daily mortality DOC 35–90. White muscle patches on abdomen. Shrimp at surface, lethargic. No single catastrophic event.
WFS: White feces floating on surface. Pale hepatopancreas. FCR >2.5.
LSS: Soft shell 48+ hrs post-moult. Mortality spikes after mass moult.
RMS Management
  • Reduce stocking density — <60 PL/m² for RMS-prone farmsPrimary preventionMost RMS farms stocked at 80–120 PL/m². Reduction to 50–60 dramatically cuts incidence.
  • Aggressive 24-hr aeration from DOC 35 — DO >5 mg/L at bottomBoyd (2023)Bottom DO deficiency is the primary RMS trigger. Bottom aeration specifically addresses this.
  • Molasses (organic carbon) 2–3×/week5–10 kg/acre
  • Partial harvest DOC 80–90 — remove 30–40% of stockReduce pond load
WFS — PCR for EHP first, then:
  • Reduce feed 20–30% + daily probiotics in feed3–5 g/kg
  • Hepatoprotective supplement (silymarin + sorbitol)2–3 g/kg · 7 days
LSS — moulting mineralogy
  • Dolomite — maintain alkalinity 100–150 mg/L50–100 kg/acreAlkalinity directly controls exoskeleton mineralisation. Most common cause in Bengal low-salinity ponds.
  • Ca + Mg mineral supplement in feed3–5 g/kgEssential in low-salinity culture (<10 ppt) where Ca & Mg are deficient.
  • Avoid abrupt salinity change >3 ppt/dayAcclimate slowlySudden change triggers synchronised mass moult → LSS mortality spike.
RMS: ২০১১ থেকে এপি ও বাংলায়। সংক্রামক নয় — কম DO + বেশি ঘনত্ব + জৈব পদার্থ। DOC ৩৫–৯০ প্রতিদিন ১–৩% মৃত্যু। WFS: EHP, ভিব্রিও, বিষাক্ত শৈবাল। LSS: খনিজ ভারসাম্যহীনতা, কম ক্ষারত্ব।

  • গুড় সপ্তাহে ২–৩ বার৫–১০ কেজি/একর
  • DOC ৮০–৯০-এ ৩০–৪০% আংশিক ধরাঘনত্ব কমান
WFS — আগে EHP PCR তারপর:
  • খাবার ২০–৩০% কমান + প্রোবায়োটিক্স খাবারে৩–৫ গ্রা/কেজি

07🟢ప్లాంక్టన్ నిర్వహణ, మురికి & బ్లూమ్ క్రాష్
Boyd & Tucker (2012): Stable phytoplankton bloom (Secchi 30–45 cm) is the biological foundation of a healthy shrimp pond — provides DO buffering, pH stability, natural feed, and competitive biosecurity. Pond bottom sludge = root cause of H₂S, ammonia spikes, and late-crop disease. Sludge accumulates from uneaten feed, shrimp waste, and dead algae.
Phytoplankton bloom management — check Secchi depth daily
  • Secchi <25 cm (too dense) — reduce feed 20% + partial water exchangeBloom thinningDense bloom = overnight DO crash risk from algal respiration. Thin with 10% exchange.
  • Secchi >60 cm (too thin) — molasses + DAP fertiliser5–10 kg + 0.5–1 kg/acre
  • BGA (blue-green algae): spot-treat Copper Sulphate only on patches0.5–1 ppm spotEntire-pond algaecide causes total bloom crash = DO disaster. Only spot-treat. Reduce fertiliser immediately.
Bloom crash — emergency response (most dangerous event)
  • Run ALL aerators at maximum immediatelyFirst actionDO crash, H₂S release, pH drop happen simultaneously within hours. Aerate FIRST — everything else second.
  • Water exchange 20–30% + Ca(OH)₂ lime50–100 kg/acre evening
  • Double-dose probioticsImmediatelyProbiotics degrade dead algae biomass at pond bottom — prevents H₂S buildup.
Pond bottom & sludge management (most important prep step)
  • Between crops: dry 7–14 days + rototill + CaO 200–300 kg/acreMandatoryFull drying kills WSSV, EHP spores, Vibrio, anaerobic bacteria AND oxidises H₂S in sediment. This single step prevents 60–70% of crop diseases.
  • Probiotics in water throughout crop (Bacillus)10⁵–10⁶ CFU/ml · every 5–7 daysBiological sludge degradation at pond bottom. Prevents anaerobic zones that produce H₂S.
  • H₂S emergency — CaO + maximum aeration + water exchange50–100 kg/acre CaORotten-egg smell at pond bottom = immediate emergency. H₂S lethal at >0.01 mg/L.
Boyd & Tucker (2012): স্থিতিশীল ফাইটোপ্লাংকটন ব্লুম (সেকি ৩০–৪৫ সেমি) সুস্থ চিংড়ি পুকুরের জৈবিক ভিত্তি — DO বাফারিং, pH স্থিতিশীলতা, প্রাকৃতিক খাবার ও জৈব নিরাপত্তা প্রদান করে। পুকুরের তলায় কাদা = H₂S, অ্যামোনিয়া বৃদ্ধি ও দেরি-ক্রপ রোগের মূল কারণ।
ফাইটোপ্লাংকটন ব্লুম — দৈনিক সেকি ডিস্ক পরীক্ষা
  • সেকি <২৫ সেমি — খাবার ২০% কমান + আংশিক জল বিনিময়ব্লুম পাতলা করুন
  • সেকি >৬০ সেমি — গুড় + DAP৫–১০ কেজি + ০.৫–১ কেজি/একর
ব্লুম ক্র্যাশ — জরুরি ব্যবস্থা
  • অবিলম্বে সব বায়ু যন্ত্র সর্বোচ্চে চালানপ্রথম পদক্ষেপDO ধস, H₂S, pH পতন একসাথে। বায়ু দিন — বাকি সব পরে।
  • ২০–৩০% জল পরিবর্তন + Ca(OH)₂ চুন সন্ধ্যায়৫০–১০০ কেজি/একর
  • দ্বিগুণ মাত্রায় প্রোবায়োটিক্সঅবিলম্বে
পুকুরের তলা ও কাদা ব্যবস্থাপনা
  • ক্রপের মাঝে: শুকানো + রোটোটিল + কলিচুন২০০–৩০০ কেজি/একরএকটি পদক্ষেপই ৬০–৭০% ক্রপ রোগ প্রতিরোধ করে।
  • সারা ক্রপ প্রোবায়োটিক্স জলে১০⁵–১০⁶ CFU/ml · ৫–৭ দিনে
  • H₂S জরুরি: কলিচুন + সর্বোচ্চ বায়ু + জল বিনিময়৫০–১০০ কেজি/একরপুকুরের তলায় পচা ডিমের গন্ধ = তাৎক্ষণিক জরুরি।

ఫైటోప్లాంక్టన్ బ్లూమ్ — రోజువారీ సెక్కి పరీక్ష
  • సెక్కి <25 cm — మేత 20% తగ్గించి పాక్షిక నీటి మార్పుబ్లూమ్ పలుచన
  • సెక్కి >60 cm — బెల్లం + DAP5–10 కేజీ + 0.5–1 కేజీ/ఎకరా
బ్లూమ్ క్రాష్ — అత్యవసర స్పందన
  • వెంటనే అన్ని గాలి యంత్రాలు గరిష్టంలో నడపండిమొదటి చర్యDO పతనం, H₂S, pH పతనం ఒకేసారి. ముందు గాలి — తర్వాత మిగతాదంతా.

  • రెట్టింపు మాత్ర ప్రోబయోటిక్స్వెంటనే

08🌾మేత నిర్వహణ, FCR ఆప్టిమైజేషన్ & జీవ భద్రత
Feed = 60–68% of variable production cost. Boyd et al. (2017, 2018) surveys of AP & Bengal farms: well-managed farms achieved FCR 1.33–1.49 vs poorly managed FCR 2.2–2.8. The difference is almost entirely feed management. Overfeeding is also the #1 cause of water quality deterioration and secondary disease outbreaks.
Feed quantity — how much and when
  • Feeding trays — 1 per 2,500–3,000 m² — check every 2 hrsNon-negotiableMost effective FCR tool. Reduce feed if >30% remains after 2 hrs. Increase if all gone in <1.5 hrs.
  • Feed 4–6 times per day — smaller more frequent mealsMin 4× dailyBoyd (2024): more frequent smaller feeds improve FCR by 0.2–0.3 points vs 2× feeding.
  • STOP feeding immediately: DO <4, heavy rain, mass moult, chemical treatment, daily mortality >30%Immediate stopFeeding stressed shrimp wastes feed and worsens water quality with zero benefit to growth.
Feed additives — apply every day throughout crop
  • Vitamin C (stabilised ascorbate)2 g/kg dailyOxidative stress reduction, moulting support, immune enhancement. Use stabilised form (vitamin C phosphate).
  • Probiotics in feed (Bacillus + Lactobacillus)3–5 g/kg dailyMix with oil for binding. Gut Vibrio suppression + FCR improvement.
  • β-glucan immunostimulant (DOC 15 onwards)3 g/kg dailyMost cost-effective immunity supplement for L. vannamei. Continue throughout crop.
  • Organic acid (butyric/formic)2–3 g/kg dailyEHP suppression + Vibrio gut control + FCR improvement. Must be daily.
Biosecurity — non-negotiable daily practices
  • Footwear BKC foot bath at every pond entryAll staff & visitors
  • Dedicated equipment per pond — nets, aerators, bucketsNo sharing without disinfectionShared equipment = #1 WSSV and EHP inter-pond transmission vector.
  • Written daily pond log — DO, pH, feed, mortality, all inputsEvery dayRequired for MPEDA/ASC certification. Identifies disease patterns across crops. Tracks withdrawal periods.
খাবার = মোট পরিবর্তনশীল খরচের ৬০–৬৮%। Boyd et al. (2017, 2018) এপি ও বাংলার সর্বেক্ষণে ভালো ব্যবস্থাপিত খামারে FCR ১.৩৩–১.৪৯ বনাম খারাপ ব্যবস্থাপনায় ২.২–২.৮। অতিরিক্ত খাবার জলমান বিপর্যয়ের প্রধান কারণ।
খাদ্য পরিমাণ — কতটুকু এবং কখন
  • ফিডিং ট্রে — প্রতি ২,৫০০–৩,০০০ m² এ ১টি · প্রতি ২ ঘণ্টায় পরীক্ষাঅবশ্যইসেরা FCR উন্নয়ন হাতিয়ার। >৩০% বাকি → কমান। <১.৫ ঘণ্টায় সব শেষ → বাড়ান।
  • দিনে ৪–৬ বার ছোট ছোট খাবারন্যূনতম ৪ বার
  • খাবার বন্ধ করুন: কম DO, বৃষ্টি, মোল্টিং, চিকিৎসা, মৃত্যু >৩০%তাৎক্ষণিকচাপগ্রস্ত চিংড়িকে খাওয়ানো বৃথা — জলমান আরও খারাপ করে।
খাদ্য সংযোজন — প্রতিদিন, সারা ক্রপ
  • ভিটামিন সি (স্থিতিশীল অ্যাসকরবেট)২ গ্রা/কেজি প্রতিদিন
  • প্রোবায়োটিক্স খাবারে৩–৫ গ্রা/কেজি প্রতিদিন
  • β-গ্লুকান (DOC ১৫ থেকে)৩ গ্রা/কেজি প্রতিদিনসবচেয়ে সাশ্রয়ী ইমিউনিটি সরঞ্জাম।
  • জৈব অ্যাসিড (বিউটিরিক/ফর্মিক)২–৩ গ্রা/কেজি প্রতিদিন
জৈব নিরাপত্তা — প্রতিদিনের অনিবার্য অভ্যাস
  • প্রতিটি পুকুর প্রবেশে BKC পাদ বাথসব কর্মী ও দর্শনার্থী
  • প্রতিটি পুকুরের আলাদা সরঞ্জামজীবাণুমুক্তকরণ ছাড়া ভাগ নয়
  • লিখিত দৈনিক পুকুর লগপ্রতিদিনMPEDA/ASC সার্টিফিকেশনের জন্য প্রয়োজন।
మేత = మొత్తం వేరియబుల్ ఖర్చులో 60–68%. Boyd et al. (2017, 2018) ఏపీ & బెంగాల్ సర్వే: సరిగ్గా నిర్వహించిన FCR 1.33–1.49 vs పేలవమైన నిర్వహణ FCR 2.2–2.8. అదనపు మేత నీటి నాణ్యత క్షీణతకు #1 కారణం.
మేత పరిమాణం — ఎంత & ఎప్పుడు

  • రోజుకు 4–6 సార్లు చిన్న మేతకనిష్టం 4 సార్లు

  • సేంద్రీయ ఆమ్లం (బ్యూటిరిక్/ఫార్మిక్)2–3 గ్రా/కేజీ రోజువారీ

  • ప్రతి చెరువుకు ప్రత్యేక పరికరాలుశుద్ధి లేకుండా పంచుకోవద్దు
